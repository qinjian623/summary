It traces the historical path, beginning with strict anti-homosexual laws in early colonial America, through the rise of McCarthyism and the Red Scare, which associated homosexuality with communism and led to a strong wave of anti-gay sentiment.

However, this oppression sparked the beginning of the LGBTQ+ rights movement in the 1960s and 1970s.
