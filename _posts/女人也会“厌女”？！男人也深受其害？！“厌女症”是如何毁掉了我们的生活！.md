**Podcast总结：**

**标题：** 女人也会“厌女”？！男人也深受其害？！“厌女症”是如何毁掉了我们的生活！

**内容概要：**
1. **厌女症的起源和本质**：
   - 美国心理学家认为人类的精神疾病本质上源自人际关系问题，而“厌女症”正是这种关系中的主要障碍。日本社会学家上野千鹤子的《验女症》一书阐明了社会中的厌女现象。厌女症的核心是通过社会化过程中男性视女性为性工具、角色化的过程，进而影响人际关系，造成深层次的痛苦。
   
2. **男性的厌女症表现**：
   - 许多男性虽然表现出厌女症，但依然喜欢女性。这种现象像巴甫洛夫的狗实验一样，男性喜欢女性并非因为喜爱她们，而是因为她们代表了男性性欲的满足。然而，这些男性往往深受同性恐惧的困扰，甚至恐同。男性与男性之间的关系并非通过欲望关系建立，而是通过相互认同为性主体来巩固。

3. **女性的厌女症表现**：
   - 女性的厌女症更加复杂，表现在女性自我意识的内化上。在父权社会中，女性常常意识到自己在社会中的低位，形成自我否定和自我压抑。为了应对这种痛苦，一些女性可能采取贬低其他女性的方式来转嫁这种痛苦，形成了所谓的“Pick Me Girl”（选我女孩）现象。这些女性通过迎合男性群体来寻求认可，但这种做法并不能消解她们内心的痛苦。

4. **如何解决厌女症带来的痛苦**：
   - 解决厌女症的唯一途径是破除性别和权力结构的压迫。男性需要打破社会对他们的性别化恐惧，女性则需要学会接纳和爱自己。上野千鹤子认为，女性主义者的目标是认识到并反抗厌女症，争取所有被压迫者的自由。

5. **女性主义的意义**：
   - 女性主义不仅仅是为女性争取权利，更是争取每个被性别和权力结构压迫的人的自由。因此，不论性别，所有人都应当站在女性主义一边，共同推动社会的解放和自由。

**核心观点总结：**
- **厌女症**是由父权社会的性别化压迫所引发的，不仅伤害女性，也影响男性的自由。
- **男性厌女症**表现为对女性的性欲化和恐同心理，而**女性厌女症**则表现为女性对自身身份的自我压抑和否定。
- **解决厌女症**的关键在于打破社会的性别结构压迫，男女共同争取自由和平等。

**中文翻译：**

**Title:** Can Women Be "Misogynistic"? Men Are Suffering Too! How "Misogyny" Is Destroying Our Lives!

**Podcast Content Summary:**
1. **Origin and Essence of Misogyny**:
   - An American psychologist believes that all mental illnesses essentially arise from interpersonal relationships. "Misogyny" is a major obstacle in these relationships. The book "Misogyny Syndrome" by Japanese sociologist Ueno Chizuko explains how social structures and gender roles lead to misogynistic behaviors, creating significant pain in human lives.

2. **Manifestations of Misogyny in Men**:
   - Many men who exhibit misogyny still like women, which seems paradoxical but is not. Like Pavlov’s dog experiment, men do not love women for themselves but because they symbolize sexual satisfaction. These men are often also terrified of homosexuality, even though their relationships with other men are not based on desire but mutual recognition as sexual beings.

3. **Manifestations of Misogyny in Women**:
   - Women’s misogyny is more complex, often internalized as self-denial. In a patriarchal society, women realize their subordinate position and tend to repress themselves. Some women, to cope with this pain, may degrade other women, forming the “Pick Me Girl” phenomenon, seeking male approval. However, this does not solve their internal pain.

4. **Solving the Pain of Misogyny**:
   - The only way to solve misogyny is to break free from gender and power structures. Men need to overcome their fear of being feminized, and women need to learn to accept and love themselves. Ueno Chizuko argues that feminists are those who recognize misogyny and fight against it to liberate everyone from oppression.

5. **The Meaning of Feminism**:
   - Feminism is not just about fighting for women’s rights but for the freedom of all those oppressed by gender and power structures. Therefore, regardless of gender, everyone should stand with feminism to promote liberation and freedom.

**Core Ideas:**
- **Misogyny** is caused by patriarchal gender oppression, which harms both women and men.
- **Misogyny in men** involves sexualization and fear of homosexuality, while **misogyny in women** is self-repression and denial.
- The key to resolving misogyny lies in dismantling gender oppression and fighting for freedom and equality for all.
